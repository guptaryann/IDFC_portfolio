Key insights

Critical

App experience is the #1 friction point

app_general has the highest negative review volume, driven by OTP failures, login issues and crashes during bill payment. This is a journey problem, not a product problem — the underlying products are sound but the delivery layer is broken.

→ Fix priority: OTP reliability, crash on payment, login flow
High risk

Credit cards have zero positive signal

100% of credit card reviews carry negative sentiment, dominated by rewards redemption failures and billing errors. This is a retention risk on IDFC's fastest-growing product — users who got the card are churning due to post-activation friction.

→ Fix priority: Rewards flow, billing statement accuracy
Opportunity

Savings account is the trust anchor

The only product with more positive than negative reviews, driven entirely by interest rate satisfaction. This is IDFC's strongest VoC asset and is currently underleveraged in acquisition messaging and cross-sell campaigns.

→ Opportunity: Lead with savings in acquisition, use as cross-sell hook

How the pipeline works

1

Schedule trigger fires every 24 hours

n8n automatically kicks off the pipeline once a day — no manual intervention needed.

n8n Schedule Trigger
2

Scrape latest Google Play reviews

Apify's Google Play Store scraper pulls the 20 most recent reviews for the IDFC FIRST Bank app. A polling loop checks every 10 seconds until the scrape completes before moving on.

Apify · Google Play Scraper
3

Normalise into unified schema

A Code node standardises every review into a consistent format — text, rating, source, date — regardless of where it came from.

n8n Code node · JavaScript
4

AI classifies each review

Each review is sent to Groq AI (Llama 3.1) with a structured prompt. The model returns three tags per review: product (savings_account / credit_card / personal_loan / home_loan / app_general), theme (kyc / rewards / payments / app_bug etc.), and sentiment (positive / negative / neutral).

Groq API · Llama 3.1 · Zero cost
5

Store in Google Sheets database

All 7 fields — original review plus 3 AI tags — are appended as a new row in the live Google Sheet. The sheet acts as the permanent database.

Google Sheets API
6

Dashboard auto-refreshes

Looker Studio reads directly from the Google Sheet. Charts for sentiment by product, top complaint themes, and rating distribution update automatically as new data comes in.

Looker Studio · Live connection
