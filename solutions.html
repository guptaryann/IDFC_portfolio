06

Proposed Solutions

UX & product interventions ranked by expected drop-off reduction

🎯

Solutions are ordered by estimated impact. Each addresses a specific root cause identified in the journey analysis. Ideal rollout: implement 01–03 in sprint 1, 04–06 in sprint 2, then measure via A/B testing.

01
🪜

Guided Step-by-Step KYC

Break the KYC process into 3 digestible micro-steps with a clear progress indicator ("Step 2 of 3"). Reduce cognitive load by showing only what's needed per step. Eliminates the "wall of form" effect.

Expected Impact: −15% overall drop-off
02
💾

Save & Resume via Deep Link

Allow users to save progress mid-KYC and return via a unique SMS/email deep link without restarting. Crucial for Tier 2/3 users with intermittent connectivity or who need to gather documents.

Expected Impact: −30% permanent abandonment
03
✅

Real-time Document Validation

Instant feedback on document quality — blur detection, wrong document type, poor lighting — before submission. Show example "good" images. Reduces backend rejection and the frustration of restarting.

Expected Impact: −40% document errors, −25% retries
04
🎥

Video KYC — Live Agent Assist

Offer live agent support during video verification for users who encounter technical difficulties. Especially effective for first-time digital users who need reassurance and guidance in real time.

Expected Impact: −20% drop-off for Tier 2/3 segment
05
🛡️

Trust Signals & Security Indicators

Display RBI compliance badges, end-to-end encryption indicators, and data security assurances prominently at the KYC entry point and alongside document upload fields. Addresses the #3 drop-off reason.

Expected Impact: +18% trust score, −12% exit intent
06
📋

Pre-KYC Document Checklist

Show users exactly which documents they'll need — with example images — before entering the KYC flow. A "Prepare in 2 minutes" screen reduces mid-flow abandonment due to missing documents.

Expected Impact: −35% mid-flow exits
