04

Customer Segmentation

Cluster-based behavior analysis across KYC drop-off patterns

🏙️ High Completion

Tier 1 Salaried Users

KYC Completion 72%
Avg. Time 4.2 min
% of Drop-offs 12%

Metro users with stable income, high digital literacy, and prior banking experience. These users have clear mental models for KYC. Marginal UX improvements yield outsized gains here.

🏘️ Medium Completion

Tier 2/3 City Users

KYC Completion 38%
Avg. Time 8.7 min
% of Drop-offs 35%

Emerging market users with moderate digital comfort. Experience higher document confusion and longer hesitation before video KYC. Guided flows and vernacular support can double conversion.

📱 High Drop-off

First-time Digital Users

KYC Completion 18%
Avg. Time 12+ min
% of Drop-offs 53%

New-to-digital users with high trust anxiety and technical friction. This segment represents the largest growth opportunity — India's 300M+ new internet users are entering this cohort every year.

Strategic Insight

Tier 2/3 and first-time digital users represent 65% of drop-offs but also the largest growth opportunity. Targeted UX interventions — step-by-step flows, live agent support, vernacular prompts — can unlock significant conversion gains in these underserved segments.
