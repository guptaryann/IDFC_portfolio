Strategic Summary

A 15% improvement in KYC completion translates to ~24,000 additional activated accounts annually, driving substantial increases in deposits, cross-sell revenue, and long-term CLV. The ROI on UX optimization investment is projected at 8–12× within 18 months. More importantly, every user converted at KYC is a user who chose IDFC over a competitor — compounding brand equity over time.
