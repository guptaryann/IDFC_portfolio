Where the Journey Breaks

Consumer Decision Process (CDP) Framework — click each stage to expand

1

Awareness

100,000 users · Baseline
+

Channels Used

  • 📱 Social Media Ads (Instagram, Facebook)
  • 🔍 Google Search Ads
  • 📺 YouTube Pre-roll
  • 🎯 Programmatic Display
Insight: High reach but low intent filtering at this stage. Users are broadly aware but not yet committed.
2

Consideration

72,000 users  -28%
+

Touchpoints

  • 🌐 Landing Pages
  • 📊 Comparison Sites (BankBazaar, Paisabazaar)
  • ⭐ App Store Reviews
  • 💬 Social Proof / Testimonials
Insight: Users actively comparing IDFC with HDFC, Kotak, Fi, Jupiter. Competitive positioning and social proof matter here.
3

Application Start

54,000 users  -25%
+

User Actions

  • 📝 Personal Details Entry
  • 📧 Email & Phone Verification
  • 🏠 Address Information
Insight: Form length and field count directly correlate with drop-off. Friction here is measurable and reducible.
4

KYC Verification ⚠

17,280 users  -68% — Critical Drop
+

Primary Drop-off Triggers

  • 📄 Document Upload Friction (blurry, wrong format)
  • 📸 Video KYC Technical Issues (connectivity, lighting)
  • 🔒 Trust & Privacy Concerns
  • ⏱️ Time-consuming — users abandon mid-session
  • 🔄 No save & resume — restart triggers permanent exit
Critical: This is the conversion cliff. Every 1% improvement here unlocks ₹12–15Cr in annual revenue. It deserves 10x the optimization investment of any other stage.
5

Activation

13,100 users  -24%
+

Success Actions

  • 💳 First Transaction Completed
  • 💰 Initial Deposit Made
  • 📱 App Features Explored
Opportunity: Post-activation engagement drives CLV expansion. Users who transact in week 1 show 4x higher 90-day retention.
