The Invisible Barrier

Every day, thousands of potential IDFC FIRST Bank customers begin the digital account opening journey — drawn in by competitive rates, zero-fee promises, and app-store buzz.

They navigate awareness campaigns, explore products, and hit "Open Account."

Then they hit KYC. And they leave.

The Reality

This isn't just an ops problem. It's a revenue haemorrhage, a brand trust signal, and a gift to every competitor with a smoother onboarding experience.

68% Drop-off at KYC stage
₹12–15Cr Annual revenue impact per 1% recovery

The KYC drop-off isn't a fringe event — it is the single largest conversion failure point in the entire digital acquisition funnel. Every percentage point recovered here directly compounds into deposits, cross-sells, and lifetime value.

The question isn't whether to fix it. The question is: where exactly does trust break down — and how do we rebuild it before the user closes the app?

Framing the Challenge

We need to understand who is dropping off, where in the KYC flow it happens, why they abandon, and what product interventions can close that gap at scale.
